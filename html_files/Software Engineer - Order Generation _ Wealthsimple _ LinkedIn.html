Wealthsimple is a financial services company on a mission to help everyone achieve financial freedom by providing products and advice that are accessible and affordable. Engineering @ Wealthsimple

Wealthsimple has several engineering teams ranging from product focused to back office and our teams build and manage the software that powers our services. We do this using a micro service architecture and an agile approach, focused on short iterations and rigorous automated testing, deploying our code over 100 times a day. We maximize our productivity by building high-performing teams and giving them autonomy to deliver results, working very closely with product management, design and operations to make sure that our work delivers maximum benefit for our clients.

In This Role, You Are Someone Who
  • Believes that simple is better
  • Loves to tackle and solve complex problems on a regular basis
  • Takes ownership and pride in working on projects to successful completion
  • Is able to produce significant output with minimal wasted effort
  • Believes that debate, inclusivity and transparency result in better products
  • Has high standards and holds yourself and others accountable
  • Is able to teach and learn from your team. We value making each other successful!
  • Loves to dive deep into the data, tools and languages you use
Skills we're looking for:
  • Expertise with at least one of the following languages: Ruby on Rails, Python, or Java/Kotlin (a willingness to learn more is a bonus!)
  • Experience with databases (SQL or NoSQL)
  • Experience designing robust APIs
  • Knowledge of agile development process
  • A proven track record of delivering well-designed and tested software
  • The drive to ship frequently and comfortable with agile development
  • Effective communication skills and driven to work collaboratively across teams
  • Some experience with micro-services and distributed systems
