Data Engineer Analyst – Procom KW is seeking an experienced Data Engineer Analyst, someone passionate about data, for an exciting cross-functional role with one of our start up clients in the cloud-based software space.

Data Engineer Analyst – Details

Our client is offering an exciting opportunity in the hyper growth transportation technology industry. Public transit is being transformed from a system of static, scheduled fixed-routes, to a dynamic on-demand network, and you’ll be one of the pioneers shaping this transformation.

Data is at the heart of our client’s core offering, and drives the decisions they make in both their product and the services offered to their clients. A s a Data Engineer Analyst, you will be responsible for building tools and processes to collect, transform, and generate insights and reports on the data needed to make these decisions. As a key technical and analytics resource for operations managers, you’ll have a vital role in shaping the design, performance, and success of the customers next-generation product offerings.

Data Engineer Analyst – Responsibilities
  • Manage and launch new reporting capabilities: Spearhead the development of an end-to-end data management and analytics platform and new client-facing reporting dashboard. Work with operations managers to transform business requirements into concrete insights.
  • Use your creative aptitude to extract unique insights: Combine your creative thinking with your analytic skills to transform big data into actionable insights. Direct exploratory data initiatives to uncover business value using statistical and analytic techniques to drive project success and support the company reaching its corporate goals.
  • Generate and analyze operational performance reports: Build comprehensive performance analysis reports spanning rider user-experience, driver user-experience, marketing, operating efficiency, and unit economics.
  • Improve internal processes and tooling: Identify ways to further systematize internal processes, including with new analysis and visualization tools.
  • Drive continuous improvement: Collaboratively develop weekly/monthly actions to drive continuous improvements, and to achieve the company’s data objectives (e.g. build new reports, database maintenance, etc).
  • Automate: Identify areas of automation within the organization. Champion efforts to streamline processes and improve team productivity.


Data Engineer Analyst – Must Have Skills
  • 2+ years of professional experience in a data centric role: Data Analyst, Data Engineer, etc.
  • A deep and intuitive understanding of database and SQL experience
  • Scripting experience or exposure (Python, etc.)
  • Data visualization experience: Tableau
  • Knowledge and experience of backend data structures, how to structure data tables, etc. – pulling/populating data
  • Undergraduate degree in Engineering / Data Science / Computer Science / Applied Mathematics / Statistics, or related discipline


Data Engineer Analyst – Nice To Have Skills
  • App building or software engineering experience
  • Leadership/mentorship experience

As soon as possible
